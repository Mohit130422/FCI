6 Years of Experience as a Solution Provider Company.

FCI started the business in Year 2017 as a Private limited company. The goal of the company was to emerge as an solution providing company into engineering sector. Today after 6 years our strength is our Team members and we consider our employees as stake holders for lifelong relationship.

Our customers are more than 500 in NCR, Haryana & Rajasthan. FCI is considered among one of known Engineering Solution Provider partners with the World Class products representing brands like Ingersoll Rand, Haskel Pumps, Butech High pressure fittings, ENI industrial lubricants . The organization has grown to cater needs of every industry be it Process Industry, Pharma, Automobile, FMCG, White goods, heavy engineering industries, Power plants and offering solutions that provide quality solution with best ROI to its esteemed customers.
