RT Series 7.5-11 kW Oil-Flooded Rotary Screw Compressors

The RT Series is more than an integrated air system, it’s a complete air solution designed to maximize the key drivers of profitability in today’s business: Reliability, Productivity, and Efficiency.

Features

Enhanced Reliability: Advanced inlet air filtration system for dryers and a fully integrated pre-separator that prevents coolant overflow ensure trouble-free operation
Robust Design: Coolers leverage an aluminum finned tube construction and are designed to operate continuously, fully loaded, in high ambient air
Superior Noise Dampening: Anti-vibration mounts and a thoughtfully designed enclosure ensure smooth, quiet operation and a safe work environment
Quick Service Access: Easy access to all internal components facilitates maintenance. Ergonomic design eases the job of operator or service technician and enhances productivity
Ingersoll Rand Premium Coolant: Factory filled with an advanced full synthetic polyalkylene glycol (PAG) lubricant provides superior cooling characteristics, reduced wear through improved lubrication, and a longer operating life than other synthetic lubricants.
